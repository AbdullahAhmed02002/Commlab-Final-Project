My Projects

  • 30 Minute Film Festival

    • Website hosting a short video about the places we come from. Shot on phone

  • COMIX STRIP

    • COMIX STRIP: GitHub

    A six panel original interactive comic where the user is a person invited to a party who must choose how they dress. With animations and sounds, the focus was on attempting to evolve the conventions of sequential art to the dynamic space of the web page

  • Sounds Like

    • Sounds Like: GitHub

    A 3 minute sound piece aiming to take the viewer through a story via audio alone. About 4 students working on a project very close to the deadline when, suddenly, the fire alarm goes off, and the situation soon escalates. Full of twists and turns

  • VID-E-OH!!!

    • VID-E-OH!!!: GitHub

    A short, entertaining video piece integrated into an interactive web page. This tells the fictional story of the CommLab games, an annual competition where the best and brightest of NYU Abu Dhabi compete to earn extra credits for the course. Camera angles facilitate the action and mini-interviews provide a sense of history and context. Shot on phone and BlackMagic camera.
